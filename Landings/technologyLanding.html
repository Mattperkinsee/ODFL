OD Technology: Advanced Solutions to Simplified Shipping

Our advanced solutions simplify shipping.

You want your shipments to move as efficiently and as safely as possible. You want to track their progress and delivery times. At Old Dominion, we are committed to making sure that happens with the state-of-the-art technology and innovative tools that we are continually seeking.

ODFL.com | ODFL4me

Using the speed of the web to move freight fast.

Our award-winning technology offers you many ways to get information and keep up with your LTL shipments, including tracing, rate estimators and pickup scheduling. ODFL4me offers registered customers access to even more information like reports, payment, enhanced tracing and more.

Handheld Devices Keep You in Touch

Dock Yard Management System (DYM)

When managing shipments, you demand on-time and accurate information. Our technology is in place to help you know what's going on with your shipment every step of the way.

We know how important it is for you to receive on-time and accurate information about your shipments. So, we have leveraged state-of-the-art technologies to help manage your domestic and global shipments more efficiently.

Radio Frequency Identification (RFID)

Electronic Data Interchange (EDI)

This technology allows us to provide you with valuable data and information whether your shipment is domestic or global. RFID technology is now embedded in nearly every aspect of our business.

To provide you with a more effective business transaction process, we utilize EDI. By using this standardized format, EDI provides us with a method of transmitting business data from one computer to another, without the need to rekey data. Please email our EDI team if you'd like more information.

Interactive Voice Response (IVR)

Freight-Handling Technology

As part of our goal to provide premium shipment tracking services, you can now gain the latest information about your domestic and international shipments via a toll-free phone number at 877-633-7344.

To help you manage global shipments more efficiently, we leverage a number of state-of-the-art technologies. These technologies have helped us earn a sterling reputation for our on-time domestic-freight-shipping-service record to 96 percent of the United States.

Descartes System

This innovative system allows us to consolidate and swap routes. This helps Old Dominion improve productivity, determine what staffing is needed and provide more detailed routing.
